Rules/ How To Play

* Enter the names of the players and submit.(/NB For now it's a two-player game.)

* Roll the dice as many times as you want to but do so at your own risk. If you roll a 1,you score no points accumulated in that particular round and your turn is over

* If you hold before rolling a 1,the points accumulated within that round are tallied and added to your score.

* Reach your target selected before your opponent and be declared the winner. HAPPY PLAY-TIME!
